How GLP-1 treatments work

GLP-1 medications belong to a class called GLP-1 receptor agonists. In simple terms, these medicines mimic a natural hormone released by the gut after eating that helps regulate appetite and blood sugar levels. By reinforcing signals of fullness and reducing hunger, they can help lower overall calorie intake when combined with lifestyle changes.
