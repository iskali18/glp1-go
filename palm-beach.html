Palm Beach County has developed into a competitive market for GLP-1 weight loss programs, with clinics concentrated in West Palm Beach, Boca Raton, Palm Beach Gardens, Delray Beach, and Boynton Beach. The area's affluent, health-conscious demographic has driven strong demand for both traditional medical weight loss centers and concierge wellness practices offering semaglutide and tirzepatide programs.

Most Palm Beach providers offer hybrid models combining telehealth consultations with optional in-person visits, making treatment accessible across the county's sprawling geography. Monthly program costs typically range from $280-$800 depending on medication type (compounded versus brand-name), dosage, and level of support included. Several clinics report 4-6 week wait times for initial consultations during peak season (January through March), when seasonal residents return and New Year's resolutions drive demand spikes.

The market skews heavily toward self-pay programs, though some providers accept insurance for office visits while patients pay separately for medication. Telehealth-first providers have gained significant market share by offering same-day virtual consultations, home delivery, and aggressive pricing on compounded medications—some as low as $69 per week. The clinics below represent the spectrum of approaches available, from traditional physician-led medical practices emphasizing comprehensive lab work and nutrition counseling, to streamlined telehealth services focused primarily on medication management and convenience.
